What is NDIS?

The National Disability Insurance Scheme (NDIS) is Australia's national scheme for people with disability. It provides funding directly to individuals to support their specific needs and goals, empowering them to live fulfilling lives.

  • Personalized support plans tailored to individual needs.
  • Choice and control over services, fostering independence.
  • Early intervention support for timely assistance.
  • Community participation, building inclusive environments.
